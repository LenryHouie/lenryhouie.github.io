Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 14, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Henry Louie ("I", "me", or "my") handles information in connection with the Learnling app (the "App").

Information I Collect

Learnling collects limited, anonymous usage and analytics data to help improve the app experience. This may include:

  • App performance and crash reports
  • General usage patterns (e.g. features used, session length)
  • Device type and operating system version

This data is collected in aggregate and cannot be used to identify you personally.

Information I Do Not Collect

I do not collect your name, email address, location, health data, payment information, or any other personally identifiable information.

Children's Privacy

Learnling is an educational app that may be used by children. I do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The analytics data collected is anonymous and does not include any personal details.

Third-Party Services

The App may use third-party analytics tools (such as Apple's built-in analytics). These services have their own privacy policies and I encourage you to review them.

Data Retention

Anonymous analytics data is retained only as long as necessary to improve app functionality and is not sold or shared with third parties for marketing purposes.

Your Choices

You can limit analytics data sharing through your device's privacy settings under Settings → Privacy & Security → Analytics & Improvements.

Changes to This Policy

I may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
